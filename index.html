Faq's

Do I need an account to place an order?

Yes, you can go to the signup page to create your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept both cash on delivery and credit card payment.

How can I change my shipping address?

When you checkout your order, you have the option to use the existing shipping address or add a new address.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping cost depends on the products purchased and the location specified. Therefore, to see the shipping charges please proceed with the checkout process, by selecting your items and adding your address.
